More About Us

As fellow students, we embarked on a mission to create something meaningful for our peers in rural areas. Our aim? To build a digital platform that goes beyond textbooks, offering study materials tailored to their needs. But that's not all. We envisioned a platform that also tracks skills, provides connectivity solutions, and offers support in finding employment opportunities. Because we believe that every rural learner deserves access to quality education and the tools to thrive in today's world.

FAQ

Is it really free to use Gram Jyoti?

Yes, absolutely! Gram Jyoti is completely free for all rural learners. Our mission is to make education accessible to everyone.

Can I access Gram Jyoti from my smartphone?

Yes, you can! Gram Jyoti is designed to be mobile-friendly, so you can access it anytime, anywhere, from your smartphone or tablet.

Are the study materials easy to understand?

Yes, we've made sure that our study materials are clear and easy to understand, tailored specifically for rural learners like you.

How do I know if the courses are right for me?

You can explore our courses and resources to see which ones interest you. Plus, you can always reach out to us for guidance on choosing the right courses for your needs.

Will I receive a certificate after completing a course?

Absolutely! Upon successful completion of a course, you'll receive a certificate that you can proudly showcase to demonstrate your new skills and knowledge.
